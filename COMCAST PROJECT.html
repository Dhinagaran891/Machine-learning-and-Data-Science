Month Level Chart of Complaints¶

Com_df["Month"] = pd.DatetimeIndex(Com_df["Date"]).month
In [9]:
Com_dfGrMonth = Com_df.groupby(Com_df["Month"])
In [10]:
plt.figure(figsize=(10,5.5))
MoM_Tickets = plt.plot(Com_dfGrMonth["Ticket #"].count(), color = 'green',linewidth=4, marker = 'o',ms=12)
plt.xticks(np.arange(Com_df['Month'].min(), Com_df['Month'].max()+1, 1.0),fontsize=14)
plt.yticks(fontsize=14)
plt.title("Month wise Tickets",fontsize=20,weight = 'bold')
plt.xlabel("Month No.",fontsize=15,weight = 'semibold')
plt.ylabel("Count of Tickets",fontsize=15,weight = 'semibold')
plt.show()

Extracted month number from the dates and created month level ticket count line chart. The negative slope from 4th to 5th month in the chart indicates a reduction of complaints in the 5th month. Similarly, the steep positive slope of the line after 5th month indicates that the complaints were very high in the 6th month
